Pune PMC: Smart City Excellence

Key Recommendations & Benefits for Municipal Administration

Water Crisis Management

For Pune, with increasing water scarcity, the ICCC system provides critical leak detection and demand forecasting. Implement automated alerts for zones exceeding 85% consumption to enable proactive water rationing and distribution across all 6-8 major zones including Hadapsar, Pimpri-Chinchwad, and downtown areas.

Peak Load Management

During summer months, Pune experiences severe power demand peaks. The AI-driven load balancing system intelligently distributes electricity during peak hours (5 PM - 10 PM). Smart suggestions for renewable energy integration with rooftop solar programs across municipal buildings.

Waste-to-Energy Initiative

Transform Pune's waste management with intelligent bin monitoring and collection optimization. The system tracks 16 smart bins per zone, predicts overflow situations, and enables efficient collection routes. Potential waste-to-energy conversion can power public services and reduce landfill pressure.

Air Quality Protection

Pune struggles with air quality during winter and festival seasons. The ICCC system provides real-time AQI monitoring across all zones, identifies pollution hotspots (industrial areas, highways), and recommends immediate mitigation actions to PMC officials for public health protection.

Citizen Engagement

Enable citizens to report water leaks, overflowing dustbins, and air quality concerns directly through the Citizen Portal. Track issue resolution rates and reward compliant areas. Increase civic participation and accountability across all municipal wards of Pune.

Cost Optimization

Expected financial savings: 15% reduction in water wastage, 12% energy cost reduction, 10% waste management optimization. Direct ROI for PMC through operational efficiency and reduced emergency interventions across water, electricity, and sanitation departments.
